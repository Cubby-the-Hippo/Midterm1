Skiing Gear

Before hitting the slopes, it's crucial to ensure you have all the right gear. This page outlines the essential equipment you'll need for a safe and enjoyable skiing experience. From skis to helmets, we'll walk you through the key items.

Essential Skiing Gear

Here is a look at all the essential skiing gear that you will need to stay safe and perform at your best on the mountain.

Skis

Skis are the most important part of your gear. Whether you're sking on groomed trails or in powder, the right ski will decide how it goes for you. Choose skis based on your skill level and the terrain you'll be tackling.

Helmet

A helmet is important for protecting your head from injury. Make sure it fits properly and is comfortable to wear for extended periods of time, like a couple hours.

Goggles

Good vision on the slopes is also important. Ski goggles not only protect your eyes from the sun but also from snow and wind. Look for goggles with anti-fog for a clearer view.

Poles

Ski poles are used to help maintain balance and propel yourself forward. Choose poles that are the right height for your body, and that involves usually measuring from the ground to just below your armpits.
