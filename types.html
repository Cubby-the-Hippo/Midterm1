Types of Skis

This webpage serves to explain the different types of skis and which type you should choose based on your skiing preferences and needs.

Why Choosing the Right Type of Skis Matters

Selecting the right type of skis is crucial for your skiing experience. Different skis are suited to different terrains and skiing styles, so choosing the one that matches your needs will enhance both your performance and enjoyment on the slopes.

Park Skis

Park skis are ideal for freestyle skiing and terrain parks, designed to handle jumps and tricks with ease.

Powder Skis

Powder skis are designed for deep snow, offering better floatation and control in off-piste conditions or backcountry skiing.

All-Mountain Skis

All-mountain skis are versatile and perform well on various terrains, from groomed runs to varying snow conditions.
