Types of Skis

This page will explain the different types of skis and which type you should choose based on your skiing preferences and needs.

Why Choosing the Right Type of Skis Matters

Choosing the right type of skis is crucial for your skiing experience. Different skis are suited to different terrains and skiing styles, so selecting the one that matches your needs will enhance your performance and enjoyment.

Park Skis

Park skis are ideal for freestyle skiing and terrain parks, designed to handle jumps and tricks.

Powder Skis

Powder skis are designed for deep snow, providing a wider platform to help float on powdery surfaces.

All Mountain Skis

All mountain skis are versatile and perform well on various terrains, from groomed runs to varying snow conditions.
