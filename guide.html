Mountain Guide

Understanding Ski Runs

Mountain runs are categorized by color to indicate their difficulty:

  • Green: Beginner - gentle slopes ideal for learning.
  • Blue: Intermediate - more challenging with steeper slopes.
  • Black: Advanced - steep and difficult runs for experienced skiers.
  • Double Black: Expert - extreme terrain for the most skilled skiers.

Safety Tips on the Mountain

Always prioritize safety while skiing. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  1. Wear a helmet to protect against head injuries.
  2. Stay within your ability level when choosing runs.
  3. Observe and follow all posted signs and warnings.
  4. Be mindful of other skiers and give them space.
  5. Always ski with a buddy, especially in unfamiliar areas.
